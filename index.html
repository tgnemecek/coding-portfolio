About

Hi! I'm a Brazilian Web Developer living in Toronto who is passionate about learning new things. As consequence, I've followed many different paths in life, including working in the Film Industry as Soundtrack Composer, in Video Games as Developer and Sound Designer and, finally, in Technology, as Web Developer.

I've worked as Front-End and Full-Stack Developer for a couple of professional and personal projects, and I'm always learning new stuff (currently studying Machine Learning), and I frequently apply the knowledge I gather from other areas in coding.

In my free time I love playing Video Games and having a great discussion about them with friends (I'm not sure if they like it that much though). During work, I absolutely love having a problem to solve. The realization of overcoming something you previously thought was impossible is the single best feeling in coding for me, and I always try to mantain myself in that zone where I'm always challenging myself to improve.
